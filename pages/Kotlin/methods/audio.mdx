## Audio

### Enable Audio

The `enableAudio` method enables the local microphone and starts producing the audio track.

**Example**

```kotlin
// Enable audio stream
val audioTrack = huddleClient.localPeer.enableAudio()
```

### Disable Audio

The `disableAudio` method stops the local audio stream and also stops producing the audio stream with remote peers.

**Example**

```kotlin
// Disable audio stream
huddleClient.localPeer.disableAudio();
```
