## Video

### Enable Video

The `enableVideo` method enables the local microphone and starts producing the video track.

<Callout type='info'>
  `enableVideo` method calls both `fetchStream` and `produce` method
  internally.It also return `MediaStream` which can be used to render the screen
  share stream in the UI.
</Callout>

**Example**

```kotlin
// Enable Video stream
val videoTrack = huddleClient.localPeer.enableVideo();
```

- ### Disable Video

The `disableVideo` method stops the local video stream and also stops producing the video stream with remote peers.

**Example**

```kotlin
// Disable video stream
huddleClient.localPeer.disableVideo();
```
