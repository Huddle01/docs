## Pre Requisites

Before using the Huddle01 Flutter SDK, you must have Flutter and its build tools installed on your machine.

- Install Flutter by following the instructions on this **[Official Flutter Page](https://docs.flutter.dev/get-started/install)**
- Download [Android Studio](https://developer.android.com/studio) or [XCode](https://developer.apple.com/xcode/) to go ahead with Flutter development.

After installing Flutter, verify it's installed by running the following command in your terminal:

```jsx
flutter doctor
```
